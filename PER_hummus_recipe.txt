% The Falaleeva - M^c^Glincy hummus recipe
% June 2nd, 2017

1. Blend 1 can (ca. 1.5 cups) drained chickpeas with a small amount of the water from the can.

2. Combine:  
    * 1/4 cup tahini  
    * Tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil  
    * 1 large clove garlic, peeled  
    * Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste  
    * 1 tablespoon ground cumin or paprika  
    * Juice of 1 lemon, plus more as needed  

3. Add to blended chickpeas and continue to blend until smooth paste.

4. Garnish with ground cumin or paprika.
